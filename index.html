Role Model Presentation—Women in Tech

Ada Lovelace is recognized by many as the first computer programmer for working with inventor Charles Babbage on what is now considered the first prototype of a modern computer, the Analytical Engine. Translating an Italian engineer's paper on Babbage's Analytical Engine, Lovelace added notes that amounted to three times the length of the translation. The notes contain the most well-thought-out and documented instructions for calculating a number series for the Analytical Engine and predictions of the many uses of such a machine.

About Me and This Website

Hi. I'm Brian Liu -- also known around the web as BLiu1. I am a freshman at the University of Washington (in Seattle) planning on majoring in Computer Science or Human Centered Design and Engineering.

During the fall of 2016, I am enrolled in a class called Gender & Info Tech that explores the concept of gender and its role in the history and development of information technology, as well as emphasizing the importance of diversity in the info tech industry. This is a blog where I showcase my work in the course.
